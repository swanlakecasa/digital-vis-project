Bikes and buses - London vs Beijing

This map compares the share bike station and bus station between London and Beijing. The public transports have extended the range of people’s activity. It also can reflect the range of people’s activity. Both maps show the number of stations is decrease from the center of city to surrounding city. Compare with both cities, this phenomenon is more clearly shown in London. For example, all the bike share stations in London concentrates on center of London, and no station is out of zone one. The below chart shows the bus stations distribution. Based on the chart we found the number of bus stations are center is higher than other areas.

However, the stations distribution in Beijing is kind of different. There are many reason, such as size of the city, external population, and the policies. As we know, the area of Beijing is ten times bigger than London, and the population of Beijing is two and a half times than London. The most people who are working in Beijing are from other cities. However, 65 percent people live at out of 4th ring road, and more than ten million, which number is close the half population of Beijing, people are live out of 5th ring road [2] because the salary is not proportional to the house price. In order to meet people’s needs, government makes many new policies. For instance, even though the center has higher number of share bike stations, the surrounding city also has share bike stations. The main reason is that Beijing share bike does not have a fixed parking space. When people hired the share bike, they can park it in any bike parking space. Also, out of 3rd ring road still have complete public transport stations, the number of stations does not change significantly. An interesting thing in here is that you can find some bus stations is not in Beijing. They belong to Heibei province. These may be Bus 930’s stops [3]. The most people who are in this bus at the morning rush hour are live in Heibei province but they work in Beijing.
