Behind the scenes

Taking us back to the ballet of a street as a concept to perceive the urban activity. A 3D vizualisation was developed. Each different part that is needed for a ballet performance to succeed, was linked with our everyday urban scene.

Above the scene

From the main stage, and the actors, that represents the places that we are familiar with. To the back stage and the mechanisms that run above actors, without the notice of the viewer, in a similar way that the underground and electrical network does. An amazing, complex and well organized system, which remains hidden in different layers.
